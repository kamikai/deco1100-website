Spin 2 Win

Stress Relief and Exercise!

Spin-2-Win is an exciting new product aiming to help students at the University of Queensland's St Lucia Campus with stress relief and promoting exercise. The idea is students can race on pairs of advanced, interactive exercise bikes, and will get to see a results of their race. Their data also contributes to the long term trends, which allow people to see how active and stressed students have been over the semester.

About

By using specialised exercise bikes, and advanced heart rate monitor bracelets, the Spin 2 Win project lets students at UQ St Lucia have friendly and competitive exercise sessions. Over time, results of the races are collected and displayed to visualise the health and motivation of people visitiing the campus.

But Why?

Our research indicated to us that stress is a strong theme of many student's lives. The Spin 2 Win project was conceived and designed to help students with two things: their stress, and providing an enjoyable exercise activity.
